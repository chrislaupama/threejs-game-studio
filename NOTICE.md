# Attribution And Scope

This unified skill incorporates and adapts provider-independent guidance and
assets from:

- `mintdotgg/mint-threejs-skills`, commit
  `489a71a9d50e662f99b08810267d2fc6edc08219`, under its bundled MIT license.
- `majidmanzarpour/threejs-game-skills`, commit
  `8919acca095be36ea054c866a79997fca90e8b22`, under the MIT license reproduced
  below. Copyright (c) 2026 Majid Manzarpour.

The Majid source is the upstream origin of the previously referenced locally
installed `threejs-game-director` family. Reused/adapted material includes its
director workflow, game design, gameplay architecture, game feel, procedural
modeling, graphics/rendering, technical art, UI, debugging, QA, deterministic
scaffold/testing patterns, and the three reviewer-only images in
`assets/scorecard-anchors/`.

Mint MCP templates, MCP credential/setup flows, remote synchronization, and
hosted-runtime phases were excluded. From the Majid source, Tripo, Gemini, and
ElevenLabs API clients; credential probes; provider task/download workflows;
CDN examples; mandatory external-sourcing gates; `lil-gui`; and third-party
physics-engine recommendations were excluded. Their provider-independent ideas
were rewritten around procedural Three.js content, project-owned or
user-supplied local files, browser Web Audio, dependency-free collision, and
local build/browser verification.
